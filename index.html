How Our Monthly Content Works

Simple definitions so you know exactly what is included.

Each ready-made post includes a caption, promotion or business message, CTA, suggested platform, suggested visual and simple hashtags when suitable.

Visuals may use your real photos, approved existing materials, a Howebz promotional poster or a suitable simple background.

A ready-made post does not automatically include a separately designed promotional poster.

A poster is one final designed visual, such as a square social poster, WhatsApp Status poster, new service poster, last call poster or holiday promotion poster.

Resizing the same design normally counts as the same poster unless significant redesign or rearrangement is required.

The Growth plan builds content around one main promotion direction: launch, explanation, customer question, benefits, result, booking reminder and last call.

It may include Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp Status, WhatsApp customer message, Google Business update and promotional posters.

Advertising management, ad budget, daily social media management, automatic posting, photography and video production are not included.

One request means one submitted group of related small changes—for example updating a promotion price, date, related banner and matching text together.

It does not include a full redesign, many new pages, custom coding, e-commerce, a complete booking system or large-scale product entry.
