Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to get started.

Howebz is designed for business owners who want a professional website without learning website tools.

Start building your website
Do I need coding or design skills?

No. Choose a template and fill in your business information. The layout, mobile design and basic setup are already prepared.

Can I use my own domain name?

Yes. Custom domains are supported on Pro and Pro Plus. Free and Basic can use a Howebz subdomain.

Can customers contact me directly?

Yes. Your website can show WhatsApp, phone, email, address and Google Maps. If you do not use WhatsApp, the website will use phone or email instead.

Can I change my content later?

Yes. Log in anytime to update your details, services, photos, promotions and opening hours, then preview and republish.

How fast can my website go live?

A basic website can be prepared in minutes once your business details and photos are ready.
